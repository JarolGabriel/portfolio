For this blog project, I conducted a **deep technical audit**. My focus was not
only on implementing initial best practices but also on diagnosing and
documenting critical areas to achieve excellence in performance and visibility.

**Implemented Strengths (What I currently offer):**

- **Efficient Architecture:** Utilizing **Static Site Generation (SSG)** for
  instant loading and minimal client-side JavaScript.
- **Code Quality:** Development in **TypeScript** with schema validation
  (**Zod**) for typed and secure content.
- **Modern Navigation:** Implementation of **View Transitions** for a smooth,
  app-like user experience.

**🔧 Diagnosed Improvement Areas (My action plan):**

1.  **Critical SEO:** Need to implement Meta Descriptions, Open Graph,
    Sitemap.xml, and Schema.org for advanced indexing.
2.  **Performance:** Requirement to migrate images to modern formats (WebP/AVIF)
    and configure Cache Headers and Preload.
3.  **Refactoring:** Correction of duplicate IDs in the HTML and text cleaning
    (_typos_).

This diagnosis demonstrates my ability to perform Quality Assurance (QA) and
plot a clear path toward total optimization.
